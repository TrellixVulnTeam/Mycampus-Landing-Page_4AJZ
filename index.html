What is Mycampus?

My campus is an endeavour to create a youth-oriented culture wherein students redefine their campus life for the better. It is a community of young minds looking to explore beyond academics and experience a holistic, fulfilling life as a college student.

With MYcampus, student ambassadors take on the role of representing MYn's brand ideology through campaigns, festivals, travel opportunities, mentorships and various other fun-filled events. By connecting our student ambassadors to such exciting, productive, and super cool opportunities, MYcampus aims to fulfil one goal: empowering and nurturing the youth of today.
